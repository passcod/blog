so many alone at night

Posted on Aug 16 ‘14

This poem is also tagged as twitter

so
many
alone
at night

wish
for one
to hold
tightly

to squeeze
to take
to kiss
sweetly

and feel
one’s arms
around
themselves

*heartbeat*
