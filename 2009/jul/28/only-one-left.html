Only One Left

Posted on Jul 28 ‘09

This poem is also tagged as schoolwork, roger-meyer

The door is closed.
Dusk fades to night.
I on a stool,
eyes on embers.

I remember.

Crowded valleys
Golden mountains

They arrived
Howls
Tears
Blood
Crumpled hope

I remember

My eyes cloud

I drop.
