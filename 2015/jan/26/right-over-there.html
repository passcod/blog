I am in the great unknown.

Slowly flying away
from nowhere to be seen
to where the wind will carry me.

Happy,
I think.

There is a bird beside me. It
sings softly, as softly as a bird
can, a melody I’ll forget soon.

I am in the great unknown.

You’ll get there too,
one day,
whoever you are.
